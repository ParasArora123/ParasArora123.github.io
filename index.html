WE ARE IN THE MIDST OF A DEADLY PANDEMIC
We are in dire need of personal protective equipment and
monetary donations - we will accept anything
Donate your equipment
Your donation will save lives! Please consider donating anything you can, we will accept both small and large donations and distribute them appropriately to your selected location. Although we are in need of N95-repirators, we ask you to think beyond just masks. Protective goggles, gloves, face-shields, wipes, and disinfectants are examples of other donations people suffering around the world would greatly benefit from.

We also guarantee your donation will not be lost or misused. You will be in direct contact with the organization recieving your donation who will confirm its safe delivery.

Pick a donation location
Unlike other organizations, we give you the unique opportunity to choose which location you want to see your donation be used at. We believe no matter what organization you choose, your donation will save lives. In some cases, hospitals will reach out to you describing exactly how your contribution helped and we hope you continue to stay in contact and contribute to those suffering around the world in whatever way possible.

To make your donation to a specified location, please click the button below and fill out the form. We appreciate your contribution.
